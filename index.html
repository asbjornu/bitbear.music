About

I started fiddling with music in NoiseTracker and ProTracker on the Amiga 500 in the early 90s. But first when I got my hands on Scream Tracker, Fast Tracker II and ultimately Impulse Tracker on a PC, I started producing “real” music under the alias Power of Creation.

I created the two albums Beyond Fantasy and 3rd Floor, and submitted songs to various demoscene competitions throughout the 90s. In the early 2000s, I put my musical career on the shelf until it was rebooted in 2012 by entering the Kindergarden 2012 Dance Music Competition under the new-found alias Bitbear.
