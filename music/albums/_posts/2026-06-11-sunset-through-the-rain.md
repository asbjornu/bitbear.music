"Sunset Through The Rain" was made for the demoparty [Solskogen
2020][solskogen] and was finally completed on {{ page.date | date: "%B %d,
%Y" }}.

<!--more-->

The EP features 2 tracks; the original "Sunset Through The Rain" and an
awesome remix of the same track by [Modulo One][modulo-one], as well as
beautiful cover art by my wonderful daughter, Liv.

Liv's cover art was submitted to the [TRSAC 2025 Freestyle Graphics
competition][trsac] in which it was awarded 1st place.
