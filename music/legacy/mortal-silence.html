This horrible song is shamefully published for brevity and nothing else. I had forgotten that the song even existed until I unearthed it among my old music archive on Monday, January 23rd 2023.

I’m not exactly sure when “Mortal Silence” was made, so the date is a guess, but judging from its extremely poor quality and the fact that I was still calling myself “PCi” in the module’s instrument list, I’d say it was one of my very first tracker modules made as a 14 year old sometime during March 1995.
