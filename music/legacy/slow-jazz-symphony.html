While being neither jazz, nor a symphony, I’m quite content with some parts of “Slow Jazz Symphony”. Except the bridge part, of course, which is horrible.

It took me quite a while to figure out exactly how to properly structure songs and ensure that each part served a purpose. As the untrained 15 year old musician I was when I made this, those skills were not yet developed.

When producing the song sometime during June 1995, I remember experimenting with different chords and managed to find a few that fit well together, and wrote a half-decent melody to go with them.
